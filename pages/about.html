About Amy

Amy is a budding photographer in McKinney, TX, who has an eye for design and beauty. Her first passion is photographing nature, but she loves working with people, too.

Amy works as a photographer for Design Sanctum. She is currently taking clients in the McKinney, Allen, Plano, and Frisco areas. You can visit www.thedesignsanctum.com for more information on booking and pricing.
